Bio

Hello my name is Nick, I was born on the 21st of June 1996 in a small town in upstate New York. After Highschool I wasn't sure what I wanted to do as a career so I decided to enlist in the Navy. I served for 5 years in the Navy as a Corpsman, which is the Navy and Marine Corps version of a medic/combat medic. I had been stationed in both hospitals as well as with Marine Infantry and Tank units over my 5 years, to incluide a deployment to Norway up in the arctic circle. When my contract with the Navy had started to come to it's end I knew I wanted to pursue a career in something I was passionate about, which lead me to game design at NEIT. I have been playing video games for as lomng as I can remember, starting with a gameboy color and slowly moving onto other handhelds until me and my brother got a PS2. The PS2 was my eye opener on what games can really offer and over the years they have been my main hobby and now I hope I can continue the passion I have for gaming as a career and make the games of the future.
